Toastmasters Roles

At Vanderbilt Toastmasters, you have the opportunity to strengthen your public speaking, leadership, and mentorship skills. We offer a welcoming, enthusiastic community for you to learn, grow, and succeed - all while having a blast!
All are welcome and no prior experience is necessary!

Prepared Speeches

As a Toastmaster, you'll take on interesting speaking projects in the Pathways curriculum which build your skills in preparing great speeches and delivering them.

Table Topics

At every meeting, we practice Table Topics, which is an exercise in extemporaneous speaking and thinking on your feet. You'll never be caught off guard by unexpected dinner conversation again.

Evaluations

Central to the Toastmasters philosophy is feedback - it's how we get better. Via evaluations, we encourage an atmosphere of constructive, positive feedback that helps us all grow and improve.

Leadership

We pride ourselves on effective leadership. As a Toastmaster, you'll have the opportunity to serve in impactful leadership roles, influence your community, and empower others to do great things.
